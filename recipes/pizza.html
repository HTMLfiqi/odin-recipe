Pizza Recipe

A fresh homemade cheese pizza, each bite a comforting blend of savory flavors that feels like a warm hug. It's simple joy in every slice, perfect for parties, family dinners, or sharing with friends.

Making a cheese pizza at home is easy and convenient because you can customize it with your favorite toppings, use store-bought dough and sauce for simplicity, and bake it to perfection in your own oven, ensuring a fresh and delicious meal without the hassle of dining out or waiting for delivery.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper (optional)
  • Any Toppings
    •

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (typically around 475-500Â°F)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet that's lightly coated with olive oil or cornmeal to prevent sticking.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  6. Add any additional toppings you like, such as basil, oregano, or pepperoni.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper if desired.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes to set, then slice and enjoy your homemade cheese pizza!
