Cookie Recipe

Moist chocolate chip cookie is soft and chewy, with a slightly crispy edge. It's loaded with gooey chocolate chips that melt in your mouth. Each bite is comforting and rich, leaving you wanting more.

Making chocolate chip cookies is easy! Mix flour, sugar, butter, eggs, vanilla, baking soda, salt, and chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake at 350Â°F for 10-12 minutes. Let cool and enjoy warm, gooey cookies!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  5. 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  6. 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. 2 large eggs
  9. 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
